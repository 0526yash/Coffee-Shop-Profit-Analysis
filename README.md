# ☕ Coffee Shop Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using Power BI to uncover business insights, monitor KPIs, and recommend strategies to improve revenue and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze current sales performance
- Identify high-performing and low-performing products
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Identify peak business hours
- Recommend data-driven strategies to increase revenue
- Evaluate the financial impact of proposed strategies using Scenario Analysis

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- SQL
- DAX
- Power Query

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Profit
- Average Order Value
- Best Selling Product
- Peak Sales Hour

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Identified the best-performing products.
- Identified low-performing products requiring promotional strategies.
- Determined peak customer purchasing hours.
- Compared sales across product categories.
- Analyzed overall revenue trends.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory during peak hours.
- Bundle low-performing products with best sellers.
- Launch targeted promotions.
- Optimize staffing during busy hours.
- Focus marketing on high-performing product categories.
